Insurance Policy FAQ:
- What is the deductible? The deductible is the amount you pay before coverage starts.
- What is coinsurance? Coinsurance is the percentage you pay after meeting the deductible.
- How do I add a dependent? Submit the dependent enrollment form within 30 days of a qualifying event.
- What is the out-of-pocket max? The most you pay in a plan year for covered services.
